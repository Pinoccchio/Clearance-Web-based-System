**BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF THE PROJECT / RESEARCH**

The Clearance Web-Based System is designed to digitize and automate the student clearance workflow across multiple departments and offices. The system will provide real-time tracking of clearance status, role-based access for students, department signatories, and administrators, and maintain a centralized record of all clearance transactions. It aims to improve efficiency, accuracy, and security in processing student clearances, ultimately enhancing the experience for both office staff and students while supporting streamlined institutional operations.

&nbsp;

**STATE THE SIGNIFICANCE OF THE PROJECT OR RESEARCH**

The Clearance Web-Based System project is significant as it addresses the need for a more efficient and secure clearance management process across multiple offices and departments. By automating clearance processing, the project reduces manual work, improves accuracy in record-keeping, and helps staff make data-driven decisions. It also strengthens transparency and accountability in tracking clearance transactions and ensures a smoother clearance experience for students. The system supports the institution's commitment to innovation, technology integration, and student welfare, making clearance operations more professional and effective.
