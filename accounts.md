## Notes

- All office accounts should be assigned the **"office"** role
- Department accounts (like CCIS) should be assigned the **"department"** role
- Club adviser accounts should be assigned the **"club"** role
- The admin account has the **"admin"** role
- The student account has the **"student"** role
- These accounts need to be created via the Admin User Management page or directly in Supabase
- **Office accounts** can be linked to offices in the Office Management page (`/admin/offices`)
- **Department accounts** can be linked to departments in the Department Management page (`/admin/departments`)
- **Club accounts** can be linked to clubs in the Club Management page (`/admin/clubs`)
